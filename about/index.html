About The Electric Outlet

Educational Media: Where Energy & Sustainability Meets the Next Generation

The Electric Outlet, an independent publication launched in February 2024, is driven by a mission to promote educational content focused on clean energy and the future of sustainability.

As a recent startup venture, we are committed to make the energy sector exciting and relatable. We cover the latest lesser-known stories in Energy & Sustainability—because the most exciting news often flies under the radar ✈

🔋 Energy & Sustainability: Our weekly newsletter delivers the latest highlights and top stories in the energy sector, focusing on the trends and innovations that are often overlooked but matter the most.

🔋 Magazines with a Twist: Every month, explore our magazine issues packed with fresh perspectives on energy's impact across various industries and lifestyles. Whether it's the intersection of technology and transportation or the surprising links between energy and fashion, there’s always something new and exciting to look forward to.

Join us, and stay ahead with content that’s as dynamic and forward-thinking as the generation we’re speaking to. Follow The Electric Outlet for an insightful and fresh way to stay connected to the future of energy!
