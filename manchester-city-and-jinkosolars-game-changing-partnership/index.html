There’s an exciting new partnership that merges the worlds of sports and renewable energy. Manchester City, a football club known for its innovation both on and off the pitch, has announced a multi-year global partnership with JinkoSolar. JinkoSolar, the world’s largest manufacturer and supplier of solar panels, is joining forces with Manchester City to promote the use of solar energy on a global scale. This collaboration is not just about branding; it’s about setting a powerful example of how sports can lead the charge in the transition to sustainable energy. Through this partnership, JinkoSolar will help Manchester City achieve its ambitious goal of becoming carbon net zero by 2030.

Who are the Key Players?

  1. JinkoSolar: JinkoSolar is a global leader in solar technology, founded in 2006 and headquartered in Shanghai, China. Renowned for its high-quality photovoltaic (PV) modules, JinkoSolar has shipped over 230 gigawatts of solar products worldwide.
  2. Manchester City FC: Manchester City FC is one of the top football clubs in the world and is known for its on-field success and commitment to sustainability. It plays home games at the Etihad Stadium and is a pioneer in green practices.
  3. The Premier League: The Premier League is England’s premier football division and one of the most-watched leagues globally. It features 20 clubs that are also dedicated to sustainability and social responsibility, supporting initiatives to promote environmental and community-focused goals.

Project Details

As part of this initiative, Manchester City plans to install ~11,000 solar panels across its Etihad Campus, which includes the City Football Academy and the Joie Stadium. These panels are expected to generate up to 4.39 megawatt hours of renewable energy annually. This is enough to power more than 2,000 homes. This move positions Manchester City as one of the largest producers of renewable energy in world football.

Powering the Future of Football

Kaitlyn Beale, Vice-President of Global Partnerships Sales at City Football Group, summed it up perfectly: “Manchester City and JinkoSolar are two leading organizations that put an emphasis on innovation in our respective fields. Both organizations are committed to creating lasting change and empowering better lives, and we look forward to JinkoSolar joining our journey as we aim to create a more sustainable future.”

This partnership is powerful because it enables global visibility and promotion for sustainability. By integrating JinkoSolar's advanced solar technology into Manchester City's facilities, the partnership showcases the practical application of renewable energy in high-profile environments. Additionally, Manchester City, with its global fan base and media presence, provides a powerful platform to promote solar energy. The visibility of JinkoSolar’s technology in a major football club’s operations helps raise awareness and drive public interest in renewable energy solutions.

Both JinkoSolar and Manchester City are leaders in their respective fields, and their collaboration reinforces their commitment to environmental responsibility. This partnership sets a precedent for how sports organizations can contribute to sustainability goals, potentially influencing other teams and leagues to follow suit.

As the world continues to face the challenges of climate change, partnerships like this are crucial. By integrating renewable energy into the fabric of one of the most popular sports globally, Manchester City and JinkoSolar are showing that sustainability isn’t just a trend, it’s the future.

But it’s not just about the energy. JinkoSolar and Manchester City are committed to educating fans about the importance of renewable energy and how they can contribute to a more sustainable future. This partnership is a great example of how two champions (one in sports and the other in renewable energy) can come together to make a real impact on the world.
